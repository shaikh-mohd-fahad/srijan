Syllabus

Introduction to Mehndi Art

  • Overview of Mehndi art and its cultural significance
  • Introduction to different Mehndi design styles
  • Basics of drawing Mehndi patterns (lines, dots, shapes)

Basic Strokes and Patterns

  • Introduction to simple floral and geometric designs
  • Practice basic strokes (lines, dots, curves)
  • Understanding how to hold and control the henna cone

Creating Symmetrical Designs

  • Practice basic strokes and patterns designs
  • Simple floral and geometric
  • Learning to create symmetrical design

Intermediate Designs

  • Introduction to more complex patterns (intricate floral motifs, paisleys)
  • Incorporating dots, lines, and shading to add depth
  • Practice creating designs for different body parts (hands, feet, arms)

Advanced Patterns and Techniques

  • Mastery of intricate designs (fine lines, detailed patterns)
  • Understanding negative space and balance in design composition
  • Practice combining various elements to create fusion designs

Bridal Mehndi Designs

  • Introduction to bridal mehndi styles and traditions
  • Practice creating elaborate bridal patterns (including dulha-dulhan designs)
  • Understanding the significance of different motifs in bridal mehndi

Arabic Mehndi Styles

  • Introduction to Arabic mehndi styles (bold outlines, floral motifs)
  • Practice creating Arabic-inspired designs
  • Understanding the flow and symmetry in Arabic mehndi patterns

Indian Mehndi Styles

  • Introduction to Indian mehndi styles (intricate patterns, peacock motifs)
  • Practice creating Indian-inspired designs
  • Incorporating traditional Indian motifs and symbols

Moroccan Mehndi Styles

  • Introduction to Moroccan mehndi styles (geometric patterns, bold lines)
  • Practice creating Moroccan-inspired designs
  • Understanding the cultural significance of Moroccan mehndi

Special Occasion Designs

  • Designing mehndi for special occasions (festivals, holidays)
  • Practice creating themed mehndi designs
  • Incorporating personalization and customization for clients

Business and Marketing Skills

  • Setting up a mehndi business (pricing, packaging)
  • Client consultations and bookings
  • Marketing and branding strategies for mehndi artists

Final Projects and Portfolio Development

  • Showcasing skills learned throughout the course
  • Creating a portfolio of designs
  • Receiving feedback and tips for further improvement
